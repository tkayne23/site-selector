Online Help:

The Residential Comp Finder application can be used by citizens, real estate listing agents and appraisers to find comparable sales along with related property tax and assessment information. In addition, the application also combines their property search with key community demographics. It is a single application that can be deployed by government agencies and accessed on desktops, tablets, and smartphone devices.

Finding Information

Users of the Residential Comp Finder application can identify recent sales that may be comparable to others in the search area. The residential real estate information, tax assessment and demographic analysis, and reports provided in the Residential Comp Finder application facilitates better decisions. This application can also be published inside your organization for appraisers to make equitable valuation decisions.

Map Based Navigation

There are several ways to interact with the map:

1. Tap a point of interest you'd like to see
2. Hold down the shift key and drag a box on the map to zoom in
3. Hold down Ctrl+Shift to zoom out
4. Roll your mouse roller to zoom in/out
5. Click or tap on the zoom buttons to zoom in/out

Searching

The search box can be used to find an address or recently sold parcel:

1. Enter the address into the Search box
2. Enter the distance from that address within which you want to search
3. Click on any of the results in the panel or any of the items on the map to get more information
4. Use the drop-down to select reports that are available for download for further use and distribution throughout your organization
